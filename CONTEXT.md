# gRank

gRank is an ongoing, listener-facing entertainment leaderboard for Nerd Snipe episodes. Its rankings are backed by transcript evidence, but the evidence supports the fun rather than defining the product as a research tool.

## Language

**gRank**:
A playful, continuously updated leaderboard that compares Nerd Snipe episodes by how quickly and how often they mention gstack.
_Avoid_: Transcript index, research tool, audit platform

**gstack mention**:
An explicit spoken reference to gstack in a Nerd Snipe episode that counts toward its rankings.
_Avoid_: Signal, accepted mention, transcript match

**first-mention ranking**:
The ordering of episodes by how soon their first gstack mention occurs.
_Avoid_: Fastest signal, first signal

**mention-count ranking**:
The ordering of episodes by their total number of gstack mentions.
_Avoid_: Most signals, total signals

**No gstack**:
The result for a reviewed episode with zero gstack mentions. It remains visible in both rankings but has no numeric place in the first-mention ranking.
_Avoid_: NR, Not ranked, No accepted mentions

**Rank**:
An episode's place in the selected ranking using standard competition ranking. Equal metric values share a rank, and the next rank skips the occupied places, such as 7, 7, 9.
_Avoid_: Dense rank

**Published leaderboard**:
The latest complete ranking snapshot. Episodes still being processed do not appear until their gstack result is ready.
_Avoid_: Pending episode, review queue
